Child Protection

Your Child's Safety is Our Priority

Safe Zone
200+ CCTV Cameras
Biometric Entry
GPS Tracked Buses
Trained Security Staff

24/7 Surveillance

Campus covered by over 200 IP cameras monitored round-the-clock. All footage securely stored and reviewed regularly.

Anti-Bullying Policy

Strict zero-tolerance anti-bullying framework. Dedicated counsellors, anonymous reporting system, and peer support groups.

Parent Communication

Real-time app updates, daily SMS alerts for attendance, and 24/7 helpline for parent concerns and emergencies.

Medical Facilities

On-campus medical room with a full-time nurse, doctor visits twice weekly, and emergency response protocols in place.

Secure Access

Biometric entry for staff, visitor management system, and no-stranger policy enforced strictly at all access points.

Mental Wellbeing

School psychologist on-campus, weekly wellbeing sessions, mindfulness programmes, and stress management workshops.
